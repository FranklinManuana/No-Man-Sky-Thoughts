No Man's Sky Thoughts

Initial thoughts

No man's sky is a game that i've been playing a lot of as of lately and am hoping to become good at. The game is a procedurally generated space exploration simulator. It has a lot of interesting elements working together to provide a unique gaming experiance. First game developed by hellogames in 2016 the highly anticipated game released with lack luster reviews, failing to live up to the high expectations that both the team and fans envisioned. However the game developed and grew to live up to those expectations in due time with each subsequent update. Now going into its 5th year in operation there is a lot to enjoy and look forward to with its upcoming next generation update.

Alone and Curious

The main focus of no man's sky is exploration, a simultatious exploration of the universe and yourself. The massive expanse of the game allows for various paths of interest to be followed such as:

  • Base construction
  • Mining
  • Exploration
  • Upgrade your equipement
  • Trading
